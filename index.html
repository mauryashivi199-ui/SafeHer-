Safety Tips

Stay aware, stay safe — every single day

01

🌙 Night Travel

Always inform someone before traveling at night. Prefer well-lit, busy routes.

02

🚕 Cab Safety

Share ride details with a trusted contact. Verify driver's name before boarding.

03

📱 Phone Ready

Keep phone charged. Save emergency numbers on speed dial.

04

👀 Stay Alert

Trust your instincts. Move to a crowded area if something feels wrong.

05

🔊 Make Noise

In danger, shout loudly. A whistle or alarm app can attract attention fast.

06

🏃 Escape Plan

Always know the nearest exit. Identify safe buildings in unfamiliar areas.
